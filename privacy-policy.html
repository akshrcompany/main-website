Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 February 2025

At Akshr, your privacy is as important to us as our chai breaks. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a secure experience while using our website, products, and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with us, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, shipping address, and payment details.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device details, and browsing activity.
  • Transaction Information: Order history, payment records, and product/service preferences.
  • Communication Data: Messages, emails, and feedback shared with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We don't collect data just to let it sit idle. Here's how we put it to good use:

  • Processing Orders: To fulfill your purchases, process payments, and deliver products.
  • Customer Support: To respond to queries, assist with issues, and improve our services.
  • Personalization: To tailor recommendations, offers, and user experience.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: To protect against unauthorized access, fraud, and illegal activities.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and respond to law enforcement if required.

3. How We Protect Your Data

We take security seriously (like really seriously). Here's what we do to keep your data safe:

  • We use encryption technologies to secure transactions and sensitive data.
  • Access to your information is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • We implement regular security audits and updates to prevent vulnerabilities.
  • Your passwords? They're stored securely-we can't even see them.

4. Do We Share Your Information?

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. However, in some cases, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party partners who help process payments, deliveries, and IT services.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, court order, or to prevent fraud.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring of Akshr.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Yes, we use cookies. Not the edible kind, but the ones that enhance your browsing experience. These help us:

  • Remember your preferences and login sessions.
  • Analyze website performance and improve functionality.
  • Provide personalized content and targeted advertisements.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work properly without them.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You are the boss of your data, and you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Ask us to delete your data (unless we're legally required to keep it).
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails or disable tracking.

7. Data Retention Policy

We keep your data only for as long as necessary. Here's the breakdown:

  • Order and Transaction Data: Stored for at least 3 years for legal and tax purposes.
  • Customer Support Communications: Retained for up to 12 months.
  • Inactive Accounts: If you don't use your account for 2 years, we may delete it.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13 years old. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we'll notify you via email or a notice on our website.
