Refund & Replacement Policy

Effective Date: 1 February 2025

At Akshr, we believe in delivering excellence, whether it's a cutting-edge tech solution or a simple AkshrCard. While we'd love for every transaction to be a happy one, we understand that sometimes things don't go as planned. So, here's our official (and hopefully painless) refund and replacement policy.

1. AkshrCard Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • Order Cancellation:
    • Changed your mind? No worries! You can cancel your order within 6 hours and receive a full refund.
    • After 6 hours, you can still cancel, but unfortunately, we can't process a refund. (We'd love to, but the system doesn't let us!)
    • Refunds, if applicable, will be processed within 7 working days. Please note that the refund amount will be credited after deducting 2.36% payment gateway charges.
  • No Refund After Shipping:
    • Once your AkshrCard has embarked on its journey to you (a.k.a., shipped), it's yours forever. No refunds. No take-backs.
  • Damaged or Tampered Products:
    • We trust our delivery partners, but just in case your package looks like it survived an adventure, check the seal before accepting it.
    • If the seal is broken, **do not accept** the package! Instead, contact us immediately, and we'll work on a replacement (and maybe give our delivery team a serious talk).

2. Service Refund Policy

  • Strict No-Refund Policy on Services:
    • When it comes to services like software development, IT solutions, UI/UX design, and consultation, once you've paid, there's no going back. No refunds, no exceptions.
    • Whether it's an advance, partial, or full payment, once the transaction is done, it's final. (Trust us, we'll do everything to make it worth it!)

3. Processing Time for Approved Replacements

If, by some unfortunate twist of fate, your product arrives damaged and your replacement request is approved, we'll work our magic and send you a fresh one within 7-10 business days (stock permitting).
